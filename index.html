Nepal Federal Parliament
live projection desk

This dashboard combines proportional representation vote share with first-past-the-post seat results to show a practical federal parliament projection. PR seats are estimated from current vote share. FPTP totals use won and leading seats reported by the upstream election feed. The main focus is whether any party is approaching the two-thirds supermajority needed to amend the constitution on its own.
